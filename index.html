About Me

I am a GIS Analyst based in Portland, Oregon. I love solving problems, and challenge myself to create unique sollutions to data issues throughout my work and studies.

In my free time I like to catch up with friends, play horseshoes, eat pizza, and drink microbrews in dive bars.
